2015: The Rise of Disinfection Robots

Overview

By 2015, hospitals began adopting disinfection robots equipped with UV-C technology. These robots played a critical role in reducing infection risks in healthcare facilities.

Key Benefits

Reduced hospital-acquired infections (HAIs).
Automated disinfection of large spaces, saving time and labor.
Enhanced safety during pandemics like COVID-19.

Challenges

High deployment costs and the need for trained operators were initial barriers to widespread adoption.

Future Prospects

Disinfection robots are evolving to become more affordable and versatile, expanding their applications to public spaces like schools, airports, and shopping centers.
