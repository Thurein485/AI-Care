How Are Surgical Robots Revolutionizing Surgery?

Surgical robots enable precision, minimally invasive procedures. These robots help reduce patient pain, minimize infection risk, and shorten recovery times, all while enhancing the safety and success of complex surgeries.

Can AI Robots Provide Emotional Support in Elderly Care?

Yes, AI-powered companion robots help in elderly care, particularly for those with dementia. They offer reminders, companionship, and emotional support, thus reducing loneliness and enhancing mental well-being.
