Xenex Germ-Zapping Robot

The Xenex Germ-Zapping Robot is a UV-C light-based cleaning device used to disinfect and sanitize spaces, especially in healthcare settings. It autonomously moves through rooms, using intense UV light to kill harmful pathogens, such as viruses, and deactivate bacteria. The robot helps reduce the spread of infections, making it a valuable tool for hospitals and clinics in maintaining hygiene.

The Xenex Germ-Zapping Robot utilizes pulsed xenon UV-C light, which is proven to be effective in rapidly disinfecting surfaces and deactivating pathogens. Unlike traditional mercury UV light, xenon light is chemical-free and environmentally safe, making it a sustainable option for healthcare facilities.
