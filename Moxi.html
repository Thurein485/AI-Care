Moxi Robot

The Moxi Robot is a specialized assistant designed for healthcare facilities. It automates essential, non-patient-facing tasks, including delivering supplies, fetching equipment, and transporting lab samples, enabling healthcare staff to focus more on patient care.

Equipped with autonomous navigation, Moxi moves seamlessly through hospital environments using advanced sensors to detect and avoid obstacles. It performs logistical tasks, helping reduce the workload on healthcare providers, who can then dedicate more time to patient interactions and critical care duties.

By easing staff workloads, Moxi enhances efficiency in hospitals and reduces burnout, promoting a healthier, more productive environment. Its applications also extend to research labs and elder care facilities, where similar needs for efficient task automation and support exist.
